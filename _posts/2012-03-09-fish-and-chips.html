It does seem weird that we have travelled half way around the planet and a prominent feature of takeaway food is so familiar – fish and chips. We have been out for fish and chips on two weekend evening already. The first time was the weekend that the container arrived, we drove up the coast to Plimmerton and ate fish and chips on the beach.

It was very sweet. The chips were very nice, cooked in bran rice oil, which we are assured by the shop is very healthy. Vinegar was not in evidence however salt was very liberally applied.

The thing that is odd is the fish. I’ve noticed this before, when travelling meat – like lamb and beef stays the same (well in name at least) but all the fish names are completely weird. Anyway Hoki and Chips on the beach looking over the bay to Whitby was very nice.

Then this weekend we went to the sunset concert in Shortland park. The sky was amazing in the sunset and the locval fish and chip shop produced some very good fish and chips so we could chill on the grass and listen to the music.
